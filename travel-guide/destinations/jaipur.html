Jaipur, India

Jaipur, the capital of Rajasthan, India, is known as the "Pink City" due to its distinct pink-colored buildings in the old city area. Founded in 1727 by Maharaja Sawai Jai Singh II, Jaipur is renowned for its rich history, vibrant culture, and stunning architecture.

Culture

Jaipur, the vibrant Pink City of India, captivates visitors with its rich blend of royal heritage and lively culture. From colorful festivals, mesmerizing folk dances, and artisanal crafts to mouthwatering cuisine and grand palaces, Jaipur offers a unique and unforgettable experience.
