About Dubai

Dubai is a global city in the United Arab Emirates, known for its futuristic architecture, luxury shopping, and vibrant culture. Itâ€™s a hub for tourism and innovation in the Middle East.

Culture

Dubai blends traditional Emirati customs with modern cosmopolitan influences. Respect local dress codes in public spaces and be mindful of cultural norms during Ramadan.

Best Places to Visit

Dubai Mall

One of the largest malls globally, featuring shops, an aquarium, and entertainment.

desert-safari.jpg

Desert Safari

Experience dune bashing, camel rides, and traditional Bedouin camps.

Food Spots

Try Emirati dishes like Al Harees and Al Machboos. Popular spots include Al Fanar Restaurant and Arabian Tea House.

Travel Tips

  • Pack light, breathable clothes and modest attire for cultural sites.
  • Public transport is efficient; taxis and ride-hailing apps are widely available.
  • Respect local customs; avoid public displays of affection.
