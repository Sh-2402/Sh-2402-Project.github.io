Cape Town, South Africa

Best time

Use e‑hailing for late evenings
Start hikes early (sun & wind)
Ocean is chilly year‑round—wetsuit for surfing

Must‑do

Cableway or Platteklip Gorge up Table Mountain
Chapman’s Peak drive to Noordhoek
Boulders Beach penguins

Eat & drink

Cape Malay curry in Bo‑Kaap
Winelands day trip (Stellenbosch/Franschhoek)
Gatsby sandwich for late‑night hunger
