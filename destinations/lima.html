Lima, Peru

Best time

Use official taxi apps; avoid street hails
Reserve top restaurants weeks ahead
Mornings are best for coastal views before fog rolls in

Must‑do

Miraflores Malecón sunset walk
Historic Centre: Plaza Mayor & catacombs
Barranco street art & cafés

Eat & drink

Ceviche (lunch; fish is freshest midday)
Anticuchos (grilled skewers)
Chirimoya or lucuma desserts
