Kyoto, Japan

Best time

Buy an IC card (ICOCA/Suica) for buses & trains
Rent a bicycle for flat districts
Temples often open by 6–8am—beat the crowds

Must‑do

Fushimi Inari sunrise torii hike
Kiyomizu‑dera + Ninenzaka lanes
Arashiyama bamboo grove (go early)

Eat & drink

Matcha parfaits in Gion
Kaiseki dinner (book ahead)
Yudofu (tofu hotpot) near Nanzen‑ji
